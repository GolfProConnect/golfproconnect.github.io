Top questions

What browser should I use to operate my GPC account?

GPC is a very modern website with a tremendous amount of functionality. GPC works best with a modern browser such as "Google Chrome". The chrome browser supports GPC’s many functions extremely well.

On Mobile Android device’s the chrome browser works best also.

on Mobile iOs devices safari works just fine

GPC's updated mobile optimized site will be available for download in the app,play and windows store shortly. We will notify you via email when ready

How do I sync my PayPal account to my GolfProConnect account to accept advanced payments for online videos and lessons booked?

This is what it’s all about! Please look at the "PayPal" link in this support section.

Can I still use my GolfProConnect account to book lessons in and receive online videos for analysis, without my PayPal account linked up with my GolfProConnect profile?

Yes! Absolutely, this will be the default setting on your account, until you decide to sync your PayPal and GPC accounts to enable "advance payment", your GPC account is fully functional in every way for accepting "bookings" and "online videos" for analysis without this.

What type of PayPal account do I need to accept all types of online payments for my services?

Any account! Follow the simple steps explained in "linking my GPC account to my PayPal account"

How do I add the GPC shortcut icon to the home screen of my mobile Device?

Android
Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into your mobile browser.
Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the settings button on your phone and select “Add shortcut to Home screen.” That’s it.

iOS Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into the safari browser.
Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the “arrow” just to the left of the safari address bar at the top of the page.
Then select “add to home screen”. The GPC logo will then appear on your home screen.

How do I access the iOS live video analysis app?

Simply search GolfProconnect in the istore and download our free app, The login credentials are the same as your main GPC account. Only GPC members can avail of the app. To Understand the synergy between video analysis via your main GPC account and via the IOS app please go to the "IOS video analysis" subsection in this support section.

What is the green tick and Red Cross beside appointments on my dashboard?

By law GolfProConnect must give the amateurs the option not to prepay for a service if they wish. These red and green notifications indicate to you who have prepaid and who have not, making it easy for the pro to reconcile their PayPal account each week.

How does a client connect with me?

Clients are able to find you via the GolfProConnect "Find a Pro" section, this search displays all GPC professionals and are listed nearest the searching location to furthest away. Amateurs can also search by person or business name. Amateurs will then CONNECT with pros they work with. Add new clients one by one or import your current client database in bulk to your GPC account. Your GPC account automatically creates an amateur account for each client you enter and sends an email with login credentials and an auto login link. Once the amateur logs in to their new GPC account they will see they are already connected to you and ready to start interacting and doing business. The importing of your current client database to your GPC account is the fastest way to create an active GPC client usership.

What’s the difference between my (public) profile and my profile on my page?

The public profile is what your amateur will view, here will be information about you and your services, this is where you sell yourself to your new potential customers and direct online traffic both to and from your current golf website should you have one. This should be fully updated so your customer will want to buy one of your services or connect you as there connected pro.
