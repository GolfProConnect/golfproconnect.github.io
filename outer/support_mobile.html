Can I operate my account on any mobile device?

GPC is available on all modern mobile devices through the internet browser of that device!

We are continuously working on improving the usability of our features across all mobile platforms and operating systems.

Video uploading and analysis functions, navigating your schedule, reports and grids will all be made extremely user friendly.

The future is mobile, we got you covered!

How do I add the GPC shortcut icon to the home screen of my mobile Device?

Android
Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into your mobile browser.
Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the settings button on your phone and select “Add shortcut to Home screen.” That’s it.

iOS Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into the safari browser.
Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the “arrow” just to the left of the safari address bar at the top of the page.
Then select “add to home screen”. The GPC logo will then appear on your home screen.

Can my clients book and pay in advance via a mobile device?

Yes, your GPC pro account has full mobile payment functionality.

What format should my client’s record videos in?

For the uploaded video to best fit the analysis dashboard please record all videos in Landscape mode. Informing your amateurs of this via a GPC message would be best.

What restriction do mobile devices currently have in relation to video upload/analysis compared with my PC/laptop?

All mobile devices
Currently the “recording” of audio is not supported by any mobile browsers, so the voice over function during video analysis is limited to the PC/desktop version for the short term.

iOS
Dual analysis is available on the ios app.
Dual analysis via a mobile browser is currently restricted.

Android
Dual analysis via a mobile browser is currently restricted.
Android is an open source platform so uploading video on ALL android devices currently can be problematic. This solution is almost complete and all GPC users on ANY android device will have video upload functionality shortly.
All of these issues mentioned will be fixed when we deploy our mobile improvements update shortly.
The mobile site update is a COMPLETE revamp of the current mobile optimized site. When the update is deployed your mobile device will interact with your GPC account in an APP like fashion and you will have FULL functionality of all GPC’s account features as on the PC/laptop.

Will these restrictions be improved in the short term?

Yes! This is one of our current developments! We are implementing software that will give all users FULL functionality across all platforms and all devices.
