1. Why should I link these accounts?

Linking your GolfProConnect account with your PayPal account gives you ability to recieve payment for the services you offer on your GPC account.

2. What type of PayPal account do I need to setup to activate this facility?

GolfProConnect wants every PGA member to have the same opportunity, so we have developed the site to work with ANY MODERN type of PayPal account either PREMIER or WEBSITE PAYMENTS STANDARD no special account needed.

No vetting means every pro will get accepted and have the same opportunity, whether you are a “1st year assistant professional” or a longtime member of the P.G.A with an already successful business.

PayPal have stopped creating the older PERSONAL accounts but if you have one of these accounts simply "upgrade" via your PayPal account to a PREMIER or the STANDARD BUSINESS account, again upgrades require no vetting whatsoever.

3. How long does it take to set up a PayPal account?

As you only require the basic PayPal account you should have an account open instantly.

PayPal themselves will ask you to verify some details as a new account holder, this process is very straightforward.

4. How long does it take me to link my GPC account with my PayPal account?

Follow the instructions BELOW and this should take no more than 10 MINUTES, this is literally a few clicks.

Linking only ever has to be done ONCE.

Once complete all service's you add to your GPC account will automatically have a payment path created backend so you can be paid directly into YOUR PayPal account.

GPC does all the heavy lifting for you

5. Instructions for linking your GPC account with your PayPal account.

What happens here is very simple.

Once you have your PayPal account setup and verified, you are going to set certain PERMISSIONS on your PayPal account to allow GPC to process transactions to YOUR PayPal account on your behalf.

Here is how to do it!

  1. Log into your PayPal account.
  2. Click on PROFILE in the menu on the top right.
  3. Click - My Selling tools/preferences - at the bottom of the left hand box.
  4. Click UPDATE to the right of API ACCESS - PLEASE NOTE - (This API button will not appear in your PayPal account enabling you to proceed until you have completed PayPal's VERIFICATION process,this is between you and PayPal - OR - If you have an older PayPal PERSONAL account, simply upgrade your PayPal account and you will be able to proceed)
  5. Select option 1 - Grant API permission -
  6. As seen below you will be asked to enter a third party permission password, enter the following Password - wellthekid_api1.gmail.com

    Click LOOKUP.

  7. Tick the following boxes ONLY:
    • Use Express Checkout to process payments
    • Issue a refund for a specific transaction
    • Process your customer’s credit or debit card transactions
    • Authorize and capture your PayPal transactions
    • Obtain information about a single transaction (very important, do not click anything else)
  8. Finally click ADD - you have now set the necessary permissions to accept payments from your GPC account to your PayPal account! Almost done!

    PLEASE NOTE: You must VERIFY your bank account with PayPal before monies for your GPC services will reach your PayPal account, again this is between YOU and PayPal.

    Once you have VERIFIED your bank account proceed to step 9.

  9. To now LINK your GPC and PayPal accounts.

    Go to the SETTINGS page of your GPC account and enter your PayPal email address ie. The email address you use to LOGIN to your PayPal account.

  10. Tick the advance payment box and click SAVE at the bottom of the Page.

    Please Note: Remember only switch on the advance payment path when you have VERIFIED your bank account with PayPal, PayPal will not process the money to your account until this is complete.

THAT’S IT!

You are now all set up and ready to receive advanced payments for services, GPC handles everything else.

YES ITS THAT SIMPLE - why not go test it out now!

What do i need to do after i link my GPC and PayPal accounts

GPC does all the hard work so the only thing left for you to do is to set up the SERVICES you wish to offer for your clients to book in on your schedule.

Payment paths for each service you offer will automatically be set up for you by GPC’S software so you have nothing to worry about except setting up your services on your account.

How do I set up my Online Video Analysis service

In SERVICES create a service called “Video Upload” set your price and create. This now activates your advance payment path for your video service.

It is VITAL that you enter the words - Video Upload - exactly as you see it here when creating this service.

This means upper case V and U, all others lower case with a space between.

If you ADD in words here or do not spell it correctly this service will not set up correctly.

Amateurs will be taken to your payment page when uploading a video to you but will also have the option to send without payment.

What Is the Green Tick and Red Cross beside appointments on my dashboard?

By law GolfProConnect must give the amateurs the option not to prepay for a service if they wish these red and green notifications indicate to you who has prepaid and who has not, making it easy for the pro to reconcile their PayPal account each week.
