Here at GolfProConnect we want to make using our product as easy as we possibly can. If you have a question please get in contact with us at info@golfproconnect.co.uk.

Top 10 questions

What browser should I use to operate my GPC account?

GPC is best supported by a modern browser such as "Google Chrome". The chrome browser supports GPC’s many functions extremely well. Android and PC users should login through the google chrome browser for full functionality, On all iOS devices the safari browser will work just fine.

Why am I unable to connect with a pro?

You MUST have your address entered in your profile section for the search function to work and to connect with ANY professionals. The search results will display the distance initially according to your device's IP location, however “search by my address” is also available.

Why can I not view and book on my connected Pro's Schedule?

This Pro has deactivated this online feature, please email or message them directly to organise a lesson.

What mobile devices is GPC available on?

Your GPC account can be accessed on all modern mobile devices via the internet browser of that device! Also download our iOS video analysis app!

How do I add the GPC shortcut icon to the home screen of my mobile Device?

Android devices -

Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into your mobile browser. Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the settings button on your phone and select “Add shortcut to Home screen.” That’s it.

iOs devices -

Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into the safari browser. Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the “arrow” just to the left of the safari address bar at the top of the page. Then select “add to home screen”. The GPC logo will then appear on your home screen.

How do I access the iOS live video analysis app?

Simply search GolfProconnect in the istore and download our free app. The login credentials are the same as your main GPC account. Only GPC members can avail of the app.

GPC will continue to provide additional tools to help the amateur golfer get the most from their golf game, so watch this space.

What is the green tick and Red Cross beside appointments on my dashboard?

You always have the option not to prepay for a service if you wish by pressing “back up” at the PayPal payment page. These red and green notifications indicate to you which services you have prepaid for. Remember if the booking or video appears on your dashboard the pro has received the green tick or red cross merely notify you of the payment status.

I made a booking in error, can I cancel?

Of Course, simply locate the booking and click cancel, should you have prepaid for a service simply message your pro for a refund or an alternative date/time.

I keep getting an error message when sending a video?

This pro has the video analysis feature disabled. The pro could be a new member and not have this set up or simply not wish to receive swing videos online. Send a message and see which it is!

Can I make a booking or upload a video from any device?

Yes, any device, anytime, anywhere! Android and PC users should login through the google chrome browser for full functionality.
