How can I use GolfProconnect’s social media to my advantage?

This can be done very easily. Increased exposure to a desired customer base is the key to making any business busier and with 5 minutes a day of a clever social media strategy any GPC Pro can significantly increase their exposure to the amateur’s following GPC on various social media platforms.

GPC is free for amateurs, Amateurs as do we all love free information and advice so an extremely large number of amateurs will not just have GPC accounts to interact with their favorite professionals but they will also follow and interact with GPC via social media and this provides a huge opportunity for GPC professionals to promote themselves and their coaching business.

Here are some simple suggestions to get started:

  1. Facebook – become friends with GPC on Facebook, post comments and share videos and generally interact with GPC. Continue to do this regularly and watch your contact list and ultimately customer base grow.
  2. Twitter – Follow GPC on Twitter, Tweet comments, Retweet and generally Interact with GPC again opening you up to a huge amount of new potential clients.
  3. Youtube – This method can be of great benefit to any driven P.G.A Pro. GPC has a specific Youtube channel which we will be using almost exclusively for the purpose of promoting our GPC Pros, any GPC professional that would like to upload a video to the GPC Youtube channel simply email the clip to info@golfproconnect.co.uk and we will upload the video for you as long as it passes certain criteria. These video clips can be free tuition videos, advertising yourself or your business, a clip you thought was great and people would enjoy. As long as we feel it will benefit you, fellow Pros or amateur golfers we will upload the video clip no problem. As I said above amateur golfers love free information but also love taking tuition, we strongly feel making a short free tuition video and promoting yourself and your business within the clip will greatly increase your client base as a number of amateur golfers will watch your clip and continue to go ahead and Connect with you on GPC.
  4. Linkedin – Join and Connect with GPC on Linkedin and take advantage of our many golf business connections all around the world.
  5. GPC Public page – your GPC public page is setup so that it is extremely quick and easy for amateur golfers when viewing your profile to connect with you not only on GPC but across all social media platforms. Take the time when setting up your Profile, to follow the simple instructions and enter your social media information in the fields provided. This allows any amateur viewing your profile to click the social media icon’s and interact with you even further.

The more exposure to new potential customers the better!

We wish you every success!
