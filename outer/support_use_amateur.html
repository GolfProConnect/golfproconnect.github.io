How do I use my GPC account?

  • Fill out your profile section fully to give your connected pro’s the most information to work with when sending you special offers or information. This section is also extremely important as without your address completed fully your “find a Pro” search function will not work properly.
  • Search Pro’s either by location or Name, browse their public profile page for a list of services and prices and CONNECT with the Pro’s you would like to interact with. You must be connected to at least 1 pro to upload a video for analysis of make a lesson booking online.
  • Once you have your Profile section completed and connected with some pro’s, you are all set to send swing videos and book lessons or interact with your Pro via messaging.
  • Subscribe to GPC’S YouTube Channel by clicking the link on the footer of your account. This will give you access to free tuition videos uploaded by our GPC Pro’s.

GolfProConnect's misson is to connect Golf Pros and amateur golfers throughout the world
