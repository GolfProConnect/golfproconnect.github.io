How do I start using GPC and get busy quickly

For a forward thinking and innovative P.G.A member the possibilities to drive revenue and streamline business operations are endless, Listed below are some suggestions to get you started!

  • Firstly set up your PayPal account through your settings tab on your dashboard. Then using the support section, link your GPC account with your PayPal account and turn on advance payments and get paid in advance for all services you offer on your GPC profile.
  • Start booking ALL your current and new appointments through your GPC schedule to take advantage of the automatic SMS and email reminder service for clients, and make those missed appointments a thing of the past, this will increase your teaching revenue dramatically. This advanced booking system also alerts you of upcoming lessons or personal events you have entered, this schedule is more flexible and easier to use than your old “paper” diary and also gives you full business mobility via your mobile phone when you are out and about.
  • VERY IMPORTANT: Enter your current customer database into the clients section of your dashboard. Here you can send all your clients an email or text message inviting them to join GPC for free and connect with you. Maybe do a special introductory offer perhaps, to encourage them to send you their swing online for analysis, the novelty factor alone for the amateurs here will get your GPC pro site busy immediately.
  • Take the time to fill out your public profile page extensively, this is your business’s advert to potential customers who will view this page when searching P.G.A pros in their area, the more impressive this page is, the more amateur golfers will connect with you and start doing business with you.
  • Print out copies of your client sheet and get club members to complete the form and give it back to you, this can be incorporated as a free prize draw. This sheet when filled out will give you all the information you need from a client, which you can enter into your clients section and grow your database.
  • Once you have built up your database of clients from your club or academy, why not send them all an email or SMS from your GPC dashboard, with an introductory lesson package offer. This encourages them to start using your GPC booking system, you receive the lesson package money in advance and they receive a slightly better deal than walking into the pro shop or paying 1 by 1, everybody wins.

These are just a few way in which we see the PGA pros making the most from GPC. Every month each pro will receive their GPC mailshot, containing new ideas from other P.G.A Professionals as to how to fully take advantage of the many features available on GPC and drive their golf business forward.
