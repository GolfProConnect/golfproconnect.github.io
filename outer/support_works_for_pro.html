How GolfProConnect works for a golf pro

  • As a GolfProConnect pro your public profile will be viewable by all amateur members of GPC, your profile will show up in searches either by location or via manual search, ie. Top 10 GPC pros nearest the searching amateurs location will be brought up first, also an amateur can search you out by entering your name or business.
  • If an amateur decides they like your public profile page, they can CONNECT with you and upload swing videos for analysis or book lessons in online or message you.
  • GPC is "free" for amateurs, thus opening up our pro members to the millions of amateur golfers online.
  • Each pro has total control and freedom over their GPC account and any monies earned from business transacted through their personalised GPC site.
  • Each pro should turn their GPC site into an “online business” by linking their GPC profile to their personal business PayPal account, all which can be done through “settings” on their GPC profile.
  • Once a pro has linked his GPC profile to his PayPal account all services offered on that pros profile will be chargeable and paid directly into their personal business PayPal account.
  • GolfProConnect has no control over, services offered, prices charged or monies earned, this is a pros personalized online business. Free to conduct business however they wish.
  • A pros GPC account can also be used as "normal" without linking to PayPal, however this makes charging for services impossible.
  • GolfProConnect charges 19 pounds + Vat a month membership for access to its software and many features which will benefit every PGA Professional.

The future of how PGA Professionals conducts and operates their business is here!!
