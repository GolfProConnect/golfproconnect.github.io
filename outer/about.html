About Us

GolfProConnect is based in England. We are making it easier for Golf Pros and amateur golfers to connect.

We care about Your Golf

Our misson is to connect Golf Pros and amateur golfers throughout the world. GolfProConnect was developed by 2 PGA Professionals in 2012 as a result of years of feedback from frustrated amateurs in relation to time and money constraints, we feel GolfProConnect addresses all the issues in the modern golf environment, for both the amateur and pro.

At GolfProConnect we care about your experience with our product. We want to here feedback and suggestions from our customers. If you see something we could be doing better or a new feature you think would benefit your business, let us know!
