How do I set up my GPC account?

  • Sync your PayPal account with your GPC account to avail of advance payment for your services. Use the PayPal subsection located in this support section to help you step by step. Then go to your settings section located below your name on the top of your Dashboard to complete the Quick process and switch on advance payments. Without syncing your PayPal account with your GPC account you cannot avail of charging for online video analysis. In your settings section you can still switch off your schedule from being bookable by amateurs if you wish. You have total flexibility.
  • Set up your services that you offer, use the help link in the services section to assist you. Here you should list all your live services you offer at your club/facility plus the online video analysis price you wish to charge. VERY IMPORTANT: You must have services set up to start booking on your live schedule as you will need to select a service when booking a lesson.
  • Set up your clients section. Transfer your current client database into the fully functional GPC client section. VERY IMPORTANT: You must select a client when making a booking on your live schedule. So it is very important to input your clients into your clients section. This then gives you a complete client history of videos sent, lessons taken including all lesson notes you have ever sent specific to each client, also they will avail of your automatic SMS and email reminders. When you add a new client they will automatically recieve an email with login details for their new GPC account, thus making it quick and easy for you to build a client database of active GPC users.
  • Finally decide whether you want your schedule viewable and bookable by the amateur public. This feature can be switched on or off at your convenience in your settings section. Most professionals will select this to be open thus opening themselves up to the public for business and prepaid lesson bookings. Your last job in this case is to customize your schedule in a few simple clicks to block off the times you do not want available for appointments. This is extremely quick and easy. Please look at the schedule subsection in this support section to assist you.

Here is your roadmap to completely setup your GPC account.

  1. Request a GPC account.
  2. Click the link to subscribe.
  3. Fill out your profile fully and sell yourself via your “public profile” page. (profile section)
  4. Set up the services you offer. (services section)
  5. Set up your client database. (client section)
  6. Link your GPC account to your PayPal account and switch on advance Payment. (settings section)
  7. Decide whether to have your schedule open to the Public or not. (settings section)
  8. If yes to point 7, then customize your schedule to only offer the times you want available for booking. (schedule section)
  9. Personalize your client lesson booking confirmation and reminder emails, as well as the automatic SMS reminder. ( settings and template section)

THAT’S IT YOU ARE ALL SET UP AND HAVE TAKEN YOUR GOLF BUSINESS ONLINE

If you have any questions or problems in setting up your account fully please contact us at info@golfproconnect.co.uk and a member of our team will help you either via email or phone call.
