How do I add a new lesson to my schedule? Why am i having difficulty booking in my first lesson?

You must have clients set up in your CLIENTS section and lesson services created in your SERVICES section to be able to book a lesson on your schedule.

Why are some bookings in BLUE and some in RED on my schedule?

Appointments will be displayed in blue and lessons booked will be displayed as red on your schedule.

What is the difference between an appointment and a lesson when booking?

A lesson booking is linked to your clients profile on GPC and gets entered into the lesson history, allows you to track lessons, send lesson notes after the lesson and activates the automatic text and email service.

An appointment entry is for your personal eyes only and will simply show up as an unavailable time for an amateur to book a lesson when they look at your online schedule.

Appointment bookings can be time off, tournaments, lunch, meetings and so on. The appointment functionality is so a pro can fully customize their schedule as to what times they want available for lesson booking and what times they want blocked off.

How do I set up recurring appointments, change a lesson time or delete/cancel a lesson?

Yes your schedule/booking system is as modern and flexible as it gets. You have total control. When in lessons mode any changes will generate an automatic email confirmation to both you and your client.

How flexible is the schedule reporting?

Massively is the answer, you have total flexibility and control to review your business performance with multiple export options available too for presentations if required.

What does an amateur see when looking at my online schedule?

An amateur will only see available free space that you have made available for lessons. Any appointment or lesson on your schedule simply appears “booked” when they view your schedule online.

You also have the option in your settings to block your schedule form clients.

How does a customer cancel a lesson?

A client can simply cancel the lesson on their GPC account and the booking will automatically cancel on your Pro diary. The pro receives a confirmation email as they will when any action is taken that involves their GPC account.

Allowing amateur clients to view your schedule or not?

In the settings section of your account you will see you have the option to not allow clients to view your schedule if you so wish. This is for professional’s who wish to use the many features of GolfProConnect but do not want their amateur clients booking in online and paying in advance via the pro’s live schedule.

Selecting this option does not affect an amateur’s ability to view a pros public page, send online videos for analysis or message a pros GPC account. It also does not stop an amateur sending a swing video and paying in advance. This feature simply keeps your schedule PRIVATE.
