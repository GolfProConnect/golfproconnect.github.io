Picture this...The Lesson

It’s a fundamental shift to make golf-coaching more profitable for pros by giving improving golfers more choice about how they learn. Technological innovation has impacted most areas of golf but coaching has been left behind until now.

There’s a basic flaw in how we’ve approached golf teaching. The golfer phones to book a lesson; if he/she bothers to arrive he/she pays for a half hour of your time (let’s call it £20); you watch him/her hit a bucket of balls – correct his/her flaws; and he/she departs. Perhaps two days elapse before he/she gets to the range again... he/she’s forgotten one or two key points you made, and begins practice – grooving a whole new swing fault or something much worse into the muscle memory. So, he/she concludes the lesson hasn’t worked and that’s the last you’ll see of him/her, EVER!!.

But from now, it’s going to be different, because now there is a solution - GolfProConnect!.

Let’s rewind the previous scenario

The golfer phones to book a lesson or books across the internet via the pro’s online schedule. Before the lesson the student receives an email and text reminder of the lesson time, date etc. He/she arrives on time and ready to start. Immediately after the half hour lesson, the student is buoyant. You’ve isolated problems, shown how to correct them, it’s been seen that it works and the student considers this £20 very well-spent. The lesson ends when you show how to establish a permanent link with your GolfProConnect profile for his ultimate convenience. You send the client the lesson notes to his/her free profile, where he/she can review the lesson at his/her convenience. These notes/videos are stored on the student’s file and act as a teaching database (great for monitoring progress). Just before he/she leaves you might borrow his/her Smartphone to save the shortcut to GPC. Now, the student is connected.
