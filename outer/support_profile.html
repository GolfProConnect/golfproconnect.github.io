How do I upload a picture to my profile?

Once you have clicked on the Profile tab you will then see your profile pop up, you need to then click on “upload a new picture” button, this will take you to your computer files where you can choose a picture from your device and click open, then upload, you will find that it should now appear on your profile.

Where do I find my facebook profile address?

Sign into Facebook. On the left hand side beside your profile picture click your profile name.

In the address bar you will now see your public profile address. This is the address you need to copy into your profile page. In the image below it would be www.facebook.com/GolfProConnect.

Where do I find my linkedin profile address?

Sign into Linkedin. In the profile dropdown menu, select 'View Profile'.

You will see your linkedin public profile address below your profile picture. In the image below it would be http://www.linkedin.com/pub/golfproconnect-ltd/5a/332/65a.

What’s the difference between my (public) profile and my profile on my page?

The public profile is what your amateur will view, here will be information about you and your services, this is where you sell yourself to your new potential customers, this should be fully updated so your customer will want to buy one of your services or connect you as there connected pro.

Why is my “exact” address so important?

It’s important you put in the exact address of your club on your profile, so amateurs can easily find you. If your address is incomplete you will not show up when an amateur searches you.

How many services can I add on my site?

The amount of services you can offer are unlimited, but just be aware of how you want your public profile page to look to your clients. Too many services makes it look cluttered and untidy, between 4 and 8 services will be a professionals average amount of services offered.
