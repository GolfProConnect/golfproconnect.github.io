What are the differences between my PC and my IPAD when analyzing video?

  • This section applies when logged in to your main GPC account through a browser via your IPAD.
  • Currently mobile browsers do not support the "recording of audio" a solution is in development and you will be notified when it is available.
  • IOS does not fully support "mini screens", this is however still fully functional. Simply refresh the page post analysis and your saved mini screens on the left will appear as they do on the PC version.
  • The "color" feature is under development and this function will be deployed to your account when complete.
  • Dual analysis is available on the IOS app, The IOS app in its finished version will have the ability to save edits and send app to app as well as app to GPC account.
