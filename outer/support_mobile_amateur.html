What mobile devices is GPC account available on?

Your GPC account is fully mobile optimized version and can be accessed on all modern mobile devices via the internet browser of that device!

The mobile upgrade currently being completed will see apps available for download via the APP STORE,GOOGLE PLAY and WINDOWS STORE.

Also you can currently download the GPC iOs video analysis app via the iStore.

What format should I record videos in?

All videos should be recorded in Landscape view.

How do I send a video to a pro?

You can send a swing via any device with an internet connection. Android users please login to your account ideally through the google chrome browser.

iOs users have two options – download and use the GPC app to share swing videos with your connected pros.

To upload a swing video and pay in advance, login to your account through the mobile browser of that device.

When uploading video via my android phone, 0% displays during uploading!

You are logged in through an inferior browser or working from an older android device.

The video will upload but may take longer.

Login to your account through google chrome and a progress bar will appear during upload.

Can I book in and pay via mobile devices?

Yes.

How do I add the GPC shortcut icon to the home screen of my mobile Device?

Android -

Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into your mobile browser. Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the settings button on your phone and select “Add shortcut to Home screen.” That’s it.

iOS -

Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into the safari browser. Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the “arrow” just to the left of the safari address bar at the top of the page. Then select “add to home screen”. The GPC logo will then appear on your home screen.
