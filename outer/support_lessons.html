Where can I find my Lesson history?

You will find a full Lesson recording platform in your LESSONS section.

All upcoming lessons will be displayed here complete with their PayPal Payment status!

Your Previous lessons can be found here also.

This is where you can adjust a lesson status from COMPLETED to NO SHOW.

How do I add lesson notes to a client’s profile?

When viewing the Previous Lessons grid, in blue next to the clients name there is an ADD OR EDIT NOTES link, here you will be able to add lesson notes.

What are templates?

There are THREE differnt type's of templates you can create and save!

LESSON NOTES - These templates save you time writing the same lesson notes over and over after each lesson. For example a complete beginner's Grip, Posture or Alignment notes will be similar.All commonly used notes should be created and saved as a template.

Saved Lesson note templates are housed in your LESSONS section and can be accessed by clicking ADD OR EDIT NOTES via the "previous lessons" grid.

CLIENT MESSAGE - These templates when saved are housed in your MESSAGING section.Why not save sales and advertising message's that are sent regularly to your customers and save yourself time.

LESSON REMINDER CONTENT - This template when saved personalize's the text content contained within the automatic SMS and EMAIL lesson reminders that your clients receive.

Can a non connected amateur book in an appointment online?

No the amateur will need to connect with you to book lessons, send swing videos or interact with you in any way.

But we have made it really easy! for you to start making money.

When you ADD A CLIENT, we automatically create a GPC amateur account for them and connect you both.They receive an email form your account with an auto-login link.
