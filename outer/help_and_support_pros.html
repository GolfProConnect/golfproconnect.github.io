Can I still use my GolfProConnect account to book lessons in and receive online videos for analysis, without my PayPal account linked up with my GolfProConnect profile?

Yes! Absolutely, this will be the default setting on your account, until you decide to sync your PayPal and GPC accounts to enable "advance payment".

Your GPC account is fully functional for accepting lesson bookings and online videos for analysis without advance payment.

How do I add the GPC shortcut icon to the home screen of my mobile Device?

Android
Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into your mobile browser.
Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the settings button on your phone and select “Add shortcut to Home screen.” That’s it.

iOS
Type www.golfproconnect.co.uk into the safari browser.
Continue to the GPC sign-in page, then click the “arrow” just to the left of the safari address bar at the top of the page.
Then select “add to home screen”. The GPC logo will then appear on your home screen.

What is the green tick and Red Cross beside appointments on my dashboard?

By law GolfProConnect must give the amateurs the option not to prepay for a service if they wish. These red and green notifications indicate to you who have prepaid and who have not, making it easy for the pro to reconcile their PayPal account each week.

How does a client connect with me?

Clients are able to find you via the GolfProConnect "Find a Pro" section.

This search displays the nearest GPC professionals to the searching amateur location. Amateurs can also search by pro,academy or club/business name.

Amateurs may then CONNECT with pros they wish to work with.

When you Add a new client's Your GPC account automatically creates an amateur account for each client and notify's them via email of their new account complete with login credentials.

Once an amateur logs in to their new GPC account they will see they are already connected to you are ready to start interacting and doing business.

The importing of your current client database to your GPC account is the fastest way to create an active GPC client usership.

What’s the difference between my (public) profile and my profile on my page?

The public profile is what your amateur will view, here will be information about you and your services, this is where you sell yourself to your new potential customers and direct online traffic both to and from your current golf website should you have one.

This should be fully updated so your customer will want to buy one of your services or connect you as there connected pro.
