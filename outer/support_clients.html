Why can i not edit some client's details?

Once you add a new client with a valid email address your GPC account automatically creates an amateur account for that client and emails them their login credentials

Once that client logs in they have now taken control of their account and you will be unable to edit certain fields within their profile

You will however have the ability to add KEYWORDS for a client at any stage and these keywords are only visible to you.

Whenever your client updates their personal profile information, it will automatically update on your account.

What are keywords?

Keywords are words or terms you can assign to clients to distinguish clients and create specific customer groups.

This feature can be used to directly market to a specific group of clients. For example Titleist players, low handicappers, high spenders, regular lesson takers, never been custom fitted and so on.

This is a great way to define your clients and help you to DIRECTLY market to them during your advertising campaigns.

How do I search using keywords?

Click on the clients tab and type in a keyword you have created like you would when searching a client.

A list of all clients within that keyword group will be displayed.

How can I assign keywords to a client’s profile?

Select a client and scroll to the bottom of their profile

Enter the keyword you wish to associate to that client and click save or enter.

Please note: The Keyword has to be all one word, no spaces.You can create an unlimited amount of keywords per client

Where can I find a clients contact details?

On the clients tab once you have searched the client you are looking for, you will be able to see all their details by clicking on their profile.

How does a client connect with me?

Clients can find you via their find a pro section of their account, they can search by location,academy,club or name, once they have found you they can view your public profile and choose to connect with you.

Once this is done you will receive a message saying you have another connected client.

It is important that your public profile is completed fully, this is where you sell yourself to your potential customers, try and stand out!

What does the connect button do?

The connect button allows new clients to interact with you, send swing videos, book in lessons and message you.

GolfProConnect's misson is to connect Golf Pros and amateur golfers throughout the world
